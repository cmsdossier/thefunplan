Travel Guide to Spain

Spain, located in southwestern Europe on the Iberian Peninsula, is a country with a rich history and diverse culture. It's capital is Madrid. Known for its art, architecture and festivals, Spain gave the world famous figures such as Picasso and Gaudí. It is home to several UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including the Alhambra and Park Güell. Spain is famous for its traditions such as flamenco dancing and bullfighting.
